WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

We are characterized by enthisiasm for cycling and attention to detail.

The bike shop was founded in 1995 by Newton Laws at the Lanex train station. Of course, the range of services that we offer our customers has also expanded. Our team is made up of qualified and knowledgeable people, all of whom are active cyclists.

Our workshop is prepared for both standard repairs and larger service work. Due to our opening times, organization and stock levels, we can provide prompt assistance in most cases. Many repairs can be made within 24 hours. It is not necessary to make an appointment with us.

The management of the bicycle parking garage, rental, sale of new bicycles, spare parts and accessories as well as repairs of bicycles of all brands form our core business. In addition to our established bicycle brands, we have also been offering the construction of individual bicycle constructions for some time.
